# 🛒 Quantium Retail Analytics Case Study

## 📌 Problem

Retail businesses often struggle to understand **customer purchasing behavior** and identify **high-value segments**.

Without proper analysis, companies miss opportunities to:

* 🎯 Target the right customers
* 📦 Optimize product offerings
* 💰 Maximize revenue and retention

This project focuses on analyzing transaction and customer data to uncover actionable insights that drive better business decisions.

---

## 💡 Solution

Performed **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on retail transaction data to identify trends, patterns, and customer segments.

### Steps followed:

* 🧹 Cleaned and preprocessed raw datasets using Pandas
* 🔄 Handled missing values and corrected inconsistencies
* 👥 Analyzed customer segments based on **lifestage and spending behavior**
* 🛍️ Evaluated product purchasing patterns
* 📊 Identified key revenue-driving segments
* 📈 Created visualizations to communicate insights effectively

---

## 🛠️ Tech Used

* 🐍 Python
* 📊 Pandas, NumPy
* 📉 Matplotlib, Seaborn
* 📓 Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📊 Results

Key insights derived from the analysis:

* 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Specific **customer lifestage segments** contributed the most to total sales
* 💰 High-value customers showed **higher revenue per transaction**
* 🛒 Certain product categories drove the majority of revenue
* 🔁 Repeat customers played a crucial role in overall profitability
* 🎯 Identified customer groups ideal for **targeted marketing strategies**

👉 These insights can help businesses:

* Improve targeted marketing campaigns
* Focus on high-value customer segments
* Increase customer retention
* Drive higher revenue through data-driven decisions
